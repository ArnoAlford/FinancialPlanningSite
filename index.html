About

Turtfolio takes your current portfolio and investment goals to generate the most tax efficient way to invest, while attaining your desired asset allocation.

This site was made as a way to give back to the amazing community at r/personalfinance for helping me enter the world of investing and take control of my finances.

All logic in this site is based on the boglehead idea of tax-efficient fund placement, which can be found here: https://www.bogleheads.org/wiki/Tax-efficient_fund_placement
